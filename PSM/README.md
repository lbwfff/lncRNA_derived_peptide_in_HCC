## Spectra & PSM

This folder contains all lncPep mass spectrometry spectra and PSM annotation information found in [Jiang et al 2019 dataset](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-0987-8) and the [Gao et al 2019 dataset](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0092867419310037?via%3Dihub).
LBF_lncPep_mzML.tar.gz and TMT_lncPep_mzML.tar.gz are compressed collections of mzML files.
